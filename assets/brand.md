## 2. Positioning

AcaiFlow sits in the "effortless healthy" lane — not a clinical diet brand, not an indulgent treat brand. The story draft explicitly rejects two adjacent positions:

- **Against:** complicated diets, calorie anxiety, restriction
- **For:** real food, balance, daily lifestyle fit, "good vibes"

The word **flow** is doing triple duty: a pun on the founder-name, a wellness metaphor ("in flow"), and a frequency cue ("eat well → flow *daily*"). Lean into all three when writing copy.

## 3. Voice & tone

From the story draft, the voice is:

- **Short sentences, lots of line breaks.** Closer to social caption than brochure.
- **Lowercase-friendly headlines** (`our story`, `superfood. glow. vibe.`).
- **Triads and rhythm:** "nourishing bowls, wholesome ingredients, flavours that keep your body and spirit in flow."
- **Warm, inclusive, no jargon.** No macros, no percentages, no "clinically proven."
- **Ends on invitation, not instruction.** "Let's flow together" over "Order now."

Words that belong: *glow, flow, vibe, balance, nourish, superfood, real food, effortless, fresh, clean, fuel, energize.*

Words to avoid: *diet, low-cal, guilt-free, detox, cheat, burn, skinny.*

## 4. Visual identity (inferred from photos + menus)

### Palette
- **Menu boards:** deep berry purple + magenta/pink as primaries, warm off-white background, accents of mustard yellow and leaf green. See [menu/acai_bowls.jpeg](menu/acai_bowls.jpeg) and [menu/byo_bowl.jpeg](menu/byo_bowl.jpeg).
- **Product shots:** hero colors come from the food itself — acai purple, spirulina blue, berry magenta, coconut cream, leafy green. Backgrounds are soft pink/coral, warm brown wood, or natural foliage.

### Props & styling
- **Signature vessel:** half-coconut shell on a wooden board — see [PHY_0038.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0038.jpg), [PHY_0039.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0039.jpg). This is the brand's photo-hero shape.
- **Smoothies** are served in tall clear glasses, often photographed in-hand with a straw — see [PHY_0040.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0040.jpg), [PHY_0046.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0046.jpg).
- **Wholefood bowls** are shown in white compartmentalized takeaway containers with small dressing cups — see [PHY_0043.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0043.jpg), [PHY_0047.jpg](assets/claude/PHY_0047.jpg). This signals "grab & go, real meal."
- **Hands in frame** are a recurring motif — the brand visually says "eat with us" rather than "admire from a distance."
- A background-removed cutout exists for the hero smoothie duo: [PHY_0046_bg.jpg](assets/original/PHY_0046_bg.jpg) (also a .psd) — that's the file to reach for when compositing onto new layouts.

## 7. Coverage gaps

Worth flagging for the next shoot or for layout decisions:

1. **No green smoothies.** Heavenly Kale and Rechargeable Power Kale — two of six on the board — have zero dedicated photos. Any layout featuring all six smoothies will need new art or stock substitutes.
2. **No 1:1 wholefood bowl portraits.** The six named Signature Wholefood Bowls are only shown as a group. Menu detail pages or "bowl of the week" features would need individual plated shots.
3. **No add-on drink shots** (Honey Yuzu Lemon, Super Berry Tea).
4. **No interior / storefront / staff / branding environment photos** — only product and lifestyle. If the website needs an "About / Visit us" section, that photography is still to-shoot.
5. **No confident per-item mapping for the 9 acai bowls.** A staff review pass against [assets/claude/](assets/claude/) would let you lock down which PHY_#### goes with which named bowl.
